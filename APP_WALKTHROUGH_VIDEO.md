## Scene 1: Opening

On screen:
- Show the login page or the main dashboard.

Voiceover:

```txt
Welcome to SportzLabs Performance Lab, an AFL analytics app built to help compare teams, track player form, and identify who is improving or going backwards during the season.
```

Caption:

```txt
AFL team and player performance analytics
```

## Scene 2: Team Dashboard

On screen:
- Click Team dashboard.
- Show the Team, Stat, and Compare filters.
- Change the selected team.
- Change the selected statistic.

Voiceover:

```txt
Start on the Team Dashboard. Choose a team, select the statistic you care about, and decide how you want to compare recent performance, such as the last three games against earlier games.
```

Caption:

```txt
Filter by team, statistic, and comparison period
```

## Scene 3: Summary Cards

On screen:
- Show Team direction, Improvers, Watchlist, and Team stat rank.
- Point out green and red indicators.

Voiceover:

```txt
The summary cards give you a quick read on team form. Green indicates positive movement or top-tier ranking, while red highlights areas that need attention.
```

Caption:

```txt
Green means improving. Red means watch closely.
```

## Scene 4: Charts And Player Movers

On screen:
- Show Team Rollup chart.
- Show League Context.
- Show Player Movers.
- Click a player in Player Movers.

Voiceover:

```txt
The charts show how the team is trending by round and how it compares to the rest of the league. Player Movers highlights who is improving and who may be slipping on the selected stat.
```

Caption:

```txt
Track team trend, league rank, and player movement
```

## Scene 5: Player Detail And PAV

On screen:
- Click a player in the Stat Table.
- Show the selected stat chart.
- Show the Player PAV chart.

Voiceover:

```txt
Click any player to inspect their selected statistic by round. The PAV chart shows Player Approximate Value by round, helping you understand contribution beyond a single raw stat.
```

Caption:

```txt
Click a player to inspect round-by-round performance
```

## Scene 6: Player Comparison Screen

On screen:
- Click Player comparison.
- Show filters on the left.
- Filter by team or position.
- Tick two or more players.
- Click Compare selected.

Voiceover:

```txt
The Player Comparison screen lets you search across every team. Use the filters to narrow the list, select two or more players, and compare them directly.
```

Caption:

```txt
Select players from any team and compare them head to head
```

## Scene 7: Comparison Chart

On screen:
- Select a statistic in the comparison screen.
- Show multiple player lines on the chart.
- Change the statistic.

Voiceover:

```txt
Choose the statistic you want to compare, and the chart shows each selected player’s performance across rounds. Change the statistic at any time to explore different parts of their game.
```

Caption:

```txt
Compare selected players by any available statistic
```

## Scene 8: Closing

On screen:
- Return to the dashboard.

Voiceover:

```txt
SportzLabs Performance Lab brings team trends, player form, PAV, and comparison tools together in one place, making it easier to spot meaningful changes as the season unfolds.
```

Caption:

```txt
SportzLabs Performance Lab
```

## Quick Recording Checklist

- Log in before recording if you do not want to show the login flow.
- Use full-screen browser mode.
- Set browser zoom to 100%.
- Use a team with strong visible examples before recording.
- Select at least two players on the comparison screen.
- Keep mouse movements slow and deliberate.
- Pause briefly after each click so viewers can see the result.
